ABOUT OUR CLINIC

Bright Smile Dental is one of the premier dental clinics in Trivandrum committed to becoming the ultimate destination for all your dental problems.

Bright Smile Dental is already a favorite name to thousands of patients. We offer a wide range of clinical services including endodontics, implantology, prosthodontics, orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry, faciomaxillary surgery, and more. We provide low-cost dental treatment when it comes to Oral Health Care.

Discover a wide range of dental solutions from a large team of qualified professionals all under one roof.

BRACES

Braces or clear aligners, for example, can enhance dental alignment, correct crowding and protrusion, and offer a more pleasing smile.

CROWNS BRIDGES & VENEERS

Restoring your old teeth, crowns and veneers can improve function and occlusion while also giving you a gorgeous smile.

IMPLANTS

Dental implants offer a valuable solution for people who have lost teeth as a result of periodontal disease, injury, or any other reason.

EXTRACTIONS

In cases of crowded teeth, extraction of healthy teeth may be necessary to create sufficient space for the remaining teeth to be properly aligned.

Medical & General Care!

We are the best in what we do!

At our clinic, we believe in harnessing the collective expertise of numerous dental surgery professionals to provide optimal treatment to our patients. With a proven track record of success, we offer a wide range of services, including dental implants, teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, smile correction, gap closure, fixed teeth, root canal therapy, gum surgery, braces, pediatric dentistry, laser dentistry, gummy smile correction, metal-free crowns and bridges, and wisdom teeth extraction.
